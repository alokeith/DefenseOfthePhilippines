T:0 Welcome to Tower defense template, use your mouse to drag and drop towers into the towers spots.  
Right click a tower to open its upgrade panel, then select an action to do.  
You can upgrade the tower, upgrade the bullets tower or sell the tower.

T1: In this area you are going to use archer towers, try to place some towers on the field, so that the archers can stop the incoming enemies.

T2: Now it's up to the wizards, add a few wizard towers to stop the enemy orde. Also use some archer towers and between them they will be unstoppable.

T3: You're doing very well, now it's the turn of the melee towers, these towers create a melee-type unit that can slow down enemies, ideal for combining with archers and wizards.
